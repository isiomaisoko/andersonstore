# Anderson Store Sales Performance & Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This Streamlit web application provides an interactive and dynamic dashboard for analyzing e-commerce sales performance. It empowers business owners, analysts, and sales teams to explore sales trends, track KPIs, and uncover insights that support data-driven decision-making.

## Key Features

### Upload Your Own Data
- Easily upload an Excel file containing sales records.
- The dashboard automatically reads and processes the dataset.

### Powerful Interactive Filtering
- Filter and segment performance by:
  - Product
  - Category
  - Region
  - Custom date range
- Enables focused exploration of specific customer or product segments.

### Automated KPI Calculations (Real-Time)
- Dynamically displays:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Units Sold
  - Total Profit
  - Average Order Value (AOV)
  - Profit per Order

### Interactive Data Visualizations
- Visual charts update instantly based on applied filters:
  - Monthly Revenue Trend
  - Monthly Profit Trend
  - Revenue by Product
  - Category Revenue Distribution (Pie Chart)

### Downloadable Insights
- Export cleaned and filtered datasets for reporting, sharing, or deeper offline analysis.

### Executive Summary & Business Intelligence Insights
- Automatically generated insights help decision-makers:
  - Identify top-performing products and regions
  - Spot growth opportunities
  - Detect profit margin strengths and weaknesses
  - Understand revenue trends over time

## What This Dashboard Helps You Discover
- Which products drive the most revenue
- Which categories are underperforming or dominating
- How regions differ in performance
- Whether revenue and profit are trending upward or downward
- How efficiently your business converts orders into profit
